📈 Quarterly payroll cycle
Hayley, Tom & Michael are paid on a quarterly cadence. Hayley: enter each quarter's CM — system annualises (YTD × 4 ÷ quarters entered), applies CM bands + milestone, and shows the 75% pro-rata payout (25% reconciled at year-end). Tom & Michael: enter their last 365 days (TTM) performance revenue each quarter — commission is calculated on that TTM, then divided by 4 for the quarter's payout (100% paid each quarter, no holdback). A year-end catch-up tops them up if their final TTM is higher than the quarterly run-rate. Run this once per quarter close, alongside the matching fortnightly cycle.

When activated, payroll will enter each quarter's team total GP-sold (from Zoho's "Quarterly GP Sold · Booking Closed Date" report) plus per-rep splits here. The dashboard auto-computes the pool funded (rep slice + company contribution), ramp factor, and per-person share. Close-out is disabled in beta.
